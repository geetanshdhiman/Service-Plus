The eServices portal involves integrated and seamless delivery of citizen services by district administration through automation of workflow, back end digitization, integration and process redesigning across participating sections/departments for providing services in a most efficient manner to the citizens.

In an attempt to fulfill this vision, the Government of Punjab has implemented the ServicePlus platform of NIC to provide services to citizens.
